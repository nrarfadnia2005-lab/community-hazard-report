What we handle

Community Health and Environmental Hazard Reporting System (CHEHRS) was established to handle environmental hazards that cause air, water, and noise pollution, as well as chemical spills and e-waste. These hazards pose the greatest threats to our local ecosystem and endangers the health of nearby people, thus this website was established for users to report any instances of environmental hazards near them.
